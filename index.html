About Us

A match made in heaven happened in 2011 when John, a Le Cordon Bleu trained chef, and Leigh Anne, an accomplished caterer and enthusiastic foodie met, fell in love, and got married. While continuing to pursue their individual careers, they secretly yearned to have a food establishment of their very own.
In 2020 they were blessed to leave those careers behind and retire in the beautiful town of Harrisonburg, content at gardening, quilting, and renovating their 1930â€™s home. At the same time, they met their phenomenal neighbors and community and started entertaining and helping with casual functions on Ott Street. Ah ha, why not do the same thing utilizing a food truck?! ,
Their dream finally materialized! Ott Street Eats is their way of getting the thrill of feeding friends and family in a one-on-one atmosphere. They look forward to meeting many new friends at their food truck, Ott Street Eats, where good neighbors and good food meet.

Community Partners

Ott Street Eats like to support other local businesses and make connections within the community

Friendly City Food Co-Op

The Co-Op provides our truck with the freshest local produce and ingredients integral to our creations

Purple Witch Teas

We currently serve 6 different varieties of fresh brewed tea, each blend crafted by the purple witch. She provides us with many different teas with different functions including: relaxing, energizing, medicinal, and sleepy teas.

Damico Farms

Damico farms provides us with dozens of fresh eggs daily. These are used in our artisinal brioche and several different recipies.

The Friendly Fermenter

The fermenter provides loads of support and customers to our truck. We partner with them and offer discounts at eachother's storefronts!
